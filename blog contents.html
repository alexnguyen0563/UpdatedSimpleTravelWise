We’ve waited all year for December and it’s finally here! Get ready to enjoy lots of festive fun this month because even though we live in LA (where the weather in sunny and warm all year long), there are so many holiday spirit mood boosters to go around this lovely city. Here’s where we’ve chosen to spend our days and nights and where you’ll find us celebrating the holiday season..

European Style Holiday Market December 2nd, 10am-8pm & December 3rd, 10am-7pm Location: 3525 Eastham Drive, Culver City Parachute – the luxe bedding company – is hosting an over-the-top, outdoor holiday market in Culver City for the very first time. The “ski” chalet inspired winter wonderland is the perfect place to do all your holiday shopping. There will be over 40 brands there, like Beyond Yoga, OSEA, Sézane, ILIA, Our Place, and The Great, to shop from plus a hot cocoa shack, cocktails and drinks from Delola, gift wrapping and embroidery, photo opps in a Parachute sleigh bed, marshmallow roasting under cozy twinkle lights, and yes- there will be snow! The cherry on top? All proceeds from ticket sales will be donated to Baby2Baby. More details & tickets here.
