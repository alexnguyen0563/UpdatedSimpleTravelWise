Loews Hollywood Hotel


The Hollywood sign. The Hollywood Bowl. The Hills. The Academy Awards. Jimmy Kimmel Live. The Hollywood Walk of Fame. All the must-visit Hollywood classics, and a stay at Loews Hollywood Hotel puts you right in the middle of all the action. Whether it's sightseeing, business, or relaxation that brings you here, our 628 guestrooms and suites are the perfect place to unwind after a day in L.A. Our rooms offer unmatched views of the city—choose from the postcard-perfect Hollywood sign, the iconic Capitol Records building, or the stunning L.A. city skyline. Just outside our doors is the expansive Ovation Hollywood—a hive of activity where you can shop, eat, or host an event at the world-renowned Dolby Theatre, home of the prestigious Academy Awards, with catering by the acclaimed chef Wolfgang Puck.

Immerse yourself in the glitz and glamour of Hollywood when you stay at Loews Hollywood Hotel. Our prime location puts you in the heart of all the iconic attractions that make Hollywood famous. Take a leisurely stroll on the Hollywood Walk of Fame and see the stars of your favorite celebrities. Catch a live taping of Jimmy Kimmel Live and experience the energy of a live television show. Indulge in the vibrant nightlife of Hollywood, with its trendy bars, clubs, and restaurants.

Omni Los Angeles Hotel at California Plaza


Situated across the street from the Museum of Contemporary Art in Los Angeles, Omni Los Angeles Hotel at California Plaza is a posh downtown LA oasis sitting atop Bunker Hill. This sophisticated hotel features a chic cafe, coffee shop, and elegant international restaurant with patio. There's also an outdoor lap pool, spa services, and sleek fitness center. Refined rooms and suites include flat-screen TVs and 24/7 room service. Club quarters add lounge access with free breakfast and cocktails. Our hotel in downtown Los Angeles rises 17 stories and reflects the sophistication of downtown Los Angeles, California. Iconic L.A. staples like Grand Central Market, The Broad Museum and Angels Flight Railway are just a few steps from our hotel. For sports fans, the hotel is near Dodger Stadium and STAPLES Center

InterContinental Los Angeles Downtown


As the tallest building west of Chicago, the InterContinental Los Angeles Downtown forever elevates the city skyline and is paired seamlessly with a genuinely personable commitment to service excellence second to none. Located in the epicenter of Downtown and mere blocks from Staples Center and L.A. Live, the hotel leads the way as the new architectural icon of luxury hospitality. Featuring a 70th floor sky lobby and 889 guest rooms including 109 suites, there is a myriad of culinary venues: La Boucherie, a Wine Spectator award-winning steakhouse; Dekkadance, an international market experience; and Spire 73. The tallest open-air bar in the Western Hemisphere. Located atop the 73rd floor. The hotel also offers 95,000 sq. ft. of indoor/outdoor meeting and event space, an outdoor pool, an executive-style gym with classes and steam rooms, and a signature Club InterContinental Lounge.

Nestled on the iconic Doheny Drive, the Four Seasons Hotel Los Angeles at Beverly Hills is the ultimate destination for those seeking an unforgettable experience. Immerse yourself in the beauty of nature, indulge in unparalleled luxury, and create lifelong memories at this remarkable hotel.
