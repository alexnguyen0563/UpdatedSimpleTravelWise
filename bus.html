LA BIG BUS HOP-ON HOP-OFF TOUR





Big Bus Tours is a globally recognized sightseeing tour operator, and its Hop-On Hop-Off service in Los Angeles provides a convenient and enjoyable way for visitors to discover the city. It offers an immersive and flexible way to explore the city's iconic landmarks and attractions. The distinctive double-decker buses with open tops offer panoramic views, allowing passengers to soak in the beauty of Los Angeles while listening to informative commentary about the city's history, culture, and notable sites.

Key Features:

Flexible Itinerary: One of the main advantages of the Hop-On Hop-Off concept is its flexibility. Passengers can join the tour at any designated stop along the route and can disembark at any stop to explore attractions at their own pace. Buses run on a regular schedule, providing convenient options for getting around the city.

Multiple Routes: Typically, Big Bus Tours in Los Angeles operate on multiple routes, covering different areas and attractions. Common routes may include Hollywood, Beverly Hills, Santa Monica, and Downtown LA. Each route highlights unique aspects of the city, ensuring that passengers have a comprehensive experience.

Informative Commentary: The buses feature pre-recorded or live commentary available in multiple languages. This commentary provides interesting facts, historical information, and anecdotes about the various landmarks and neighborhoods encountered during the tour, enhancing the overall sightseeing experience.

Iconic Landmarks: The routes often include stops at some of Los Angeles' most famous landmarks, such as the Hollywood Walk of Fame, Griffith Observatory, Rodeo Drive, the Santa Monica Pier, and the TCL Chinese Theatre. Passengers have the opportunity to hop off, explore these sites, and hop back on when they're ready to continue.

Ticket Options: Big Bus Tours typically offers various ticket options, including single-day and multi-day passes. Some packages may include additional perks such as boat tours, walking tours, or admission to specific attractions.

Comfort and Amenities: The buses are designed for comfort, with spacious seating and the option to sit on the open-top deck for unobstructed views. Wi-Fi may be available on board, and some buses are equipped with air conditioning for passenger comfort.

Night Tours: In addition to daytime tours, Big Bus Tours in Los Angeles may offer special night tours. These tours provide a unique perspective of the cityscape when illuminated at night, often featuring stops at popular nightlife districts.

Ticketing and Boarding: Tickets for Big Bus Tours can be purchased online, at designated ticket counters, or directly from the bus drivers. Passengers receive a map, headphones for the commentary, and a wristband or ticket that grants them access to the bus for the specified duration of their ticket. For the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding routes, schedules, ticket prices, and any additional offerings, it's recommended to visit the official website of Big Bus Tours or contact their customer service directly.
